**nose**: baked fruits, toffee, oak, sweetness, sherry

**taste**: intense, sweet fruits but very strong due to its 110 proof.

**finish**: strong but smooth, sherry and nuts. very spicy.

**rating: 89**

add water to get lots of toffee and sweetness out of it.

i am really developing an affection for japanese whiskys. i had already had a nikka, yoichi, karuizawa noh and a few others and all of them were really good. unfortunately due to the fukushima tragedy a lot of destillers lost their stock and the ones that are left raise the prices sky high :/
