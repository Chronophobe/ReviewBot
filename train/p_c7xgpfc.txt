**finish neat**: medium long and dry, sherry, nuts, slightly bitter but very pleasant.

**nose water**: toffee, citrus, berrys, and vanilla

**taste water**: sweet orange, sherry, less bitter, walnuts

**finish water**: less dry, chocolate, vanilla, sweet oak, later oaky bitterness

**rating: 84**
