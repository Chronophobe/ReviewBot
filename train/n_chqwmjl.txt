plenty of folks are making some type of whiskey in texas.  balcones primarily makes corn whiskey.  corn whiskey and bourbon are very similar although bourbon is aged in new charred oak barrels and corn whiskey is aged in used barrels.  balcones has made a couple of barrels of bourbon.  garrison brothers is the most well know legit grain to glass texas straight bourbon.  bone spirits makes fitch's goat corn whiskey.  yellow rose in houston makes a legit bourbon called yellow rose outlaw (but they do source their other products).
